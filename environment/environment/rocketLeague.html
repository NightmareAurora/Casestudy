Rocket League

You've heard of soccer. You've heard of RC cars. Now, get ready for soccer but with rocket powered cars! The goal of the game is simple. Use your rocket powered car to hit the soccer ball into the enemy goal. There are usually teams of two or three, depending on which you choose.

Rocket league is the sequel to the 2008 game Supersonic Acrobatic Rocket-Powered Battle-Cars. The game recieved mixed reviews and in all, was not a major success. However, it did gain a loyal fan base.

Rocket League was praised for its gameplay improvements over it's successor, as well as graphics and its overall presentation. Rocket League became so popular that it is not an esport, with professional players participating through ESL and Major League Gaming.
